Support

We're here to help.

Make sure NotiCare has notification permissions enabled. Go to iPhone Settings → NotiCare → Notifications and turn on Allow Notifications.
Open NotiCare → tap the ⚙️ icon → Settings → Language. The change applies instantly.
NotiCare data is included in your iPhone's iCloud backup and restored automatically on reinstall.
When an event expires NotiCare asks to reschedule. You can also renew from Settings → History → ↺.
